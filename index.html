Idea

The idea is to change the experience of movie using illuminations.

Concept

Using LEDs in the cinema theatre to illuminate scene theme to stimulate mood of the people who are seeing the movie without interrupting the user experience.

Architecture

For prototype, we are using a Raspberry Pi as a computer. The RPI will have two outputs, one to a screen and another to a LED driver. Using SD card as a disk, the Pi will play the movie to screen as well as output the light animations to the LEDs.
